# Safe Turn Advisory — Content Calendar

## Posting Schedule

**Frequency:** 1 post per day, 5 days/week (Mon-Fri)
**Time:** 9:00-10:00 AM ET (peak B2B LinkedIn engagement window)
**Platform:** LinkedIn (personal profile of founder — 8x more engagement than company page)

## Weekly Pillar Rotation

| Day | Primary Pillar | Why |
|-----|---------------|-----|
| Monday | Relatable Pain | Start the week hitting the nerve — people feel business stress hardest on Mondays |
| Tuesday | Contrarian | Mid-week engagement driver — hot takes spark debate |
| Wednesday | Educational | Hump day = learning mode — people engage with actionable content |
| Thursday | Story / Scenario | End-of-week storytelling holds attention (dwell time play) |
| Friday | Perspective Shift OR Authority Insight | Alternate weekly — reflective content performs well heading into weekends |

This is a default rotation. Flex based on what's performing. The only hard rule: never repeat the same pillar two days in a row.

## Engagement Protocol (Non-Negotiable)

**Before posting (10 min):**
- Engage on 3-5 posts from target accounts
- Leave thoughtful comments (not "Great post!")
- This primes the algorithm to show your post to similar audiences

**After posting (first 90 min is critical):**
- Reply to EVERY comment on your post
- LinkedIn's algorithm judges your post's reach in the first 90 minutes
- Early engagement = exponential reach

**First comment strategy:**
- Post your first comment immediately after the post goes live
- This is where links go (never in the post body)
- Can also add context, a question, or a micro-CTA

## Content Reactivation

- Comment on your own post 8 hours after publishing to push it back into feeds
- Share a related thought or answer a question nobody asked
- This is a documented algorithm hack that works in 2025-2026
