# Safe Turn Advisory — Daily Workflow

## Morning Routine (15 min) — 8:30-9:00 AM ET

### Step 1: Engage First (5 min)
Before posting, engage on 3-5 posts from your target audience:
- Business owners posting about challenges
- Finance/advisory content creators
- Entrepreneurship thought leaders

Leave thoughtful comments that add value. Never pitch in comments.

### Step 2: Generate & Post (5 min)
- Run the content engine to generate today's post
- Review for voice and accuracy
- Post from personal profile (NOT company page)
- Post between 9:00-10:00 AM ET
- Immediately add first comment (link to site, additional context, or question)

### Step 3: First 90 Minutes (5 min, spread out)
- Reply to every comment on your post within 90 minutes
- The algorithm judges reach heavily in this window
- Thoughtful replies > quick reactions

## Afternoon Check (5 min) — 2:00-3:00 PM ET

- Reply to any new comments
- Engage on 2-3 more posts from target accounts
- Check DMs — respond to any inbound conversations

## Evening Reactivation (2 min) — 6:00-7:00 PM ET

- Comment on your own post with an additional thought
- This pushes it back into feeds for a second wave

## Weekly Review (Sunday, 15 min)

- Review which posts got most engagement
- Note which pillars performed best
- Adjust next week's rotation if needed
- Write/schedule 2-3 posts in advance for busy days
